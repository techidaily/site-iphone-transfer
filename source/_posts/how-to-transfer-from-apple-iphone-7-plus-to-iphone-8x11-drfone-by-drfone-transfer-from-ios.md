Step 2. Using an USB, connect the Apple iPhone 7 Plus to your PC and wait until iTunes automatically detects it. You will be able to view your Apple iPhone 7 Plus under the “Devices” tab.

Step 3. Click Apple iPhone 7 Plus to view all the options at the left hand side of the iTunes interface. Select “**Backup Now**” to backup all the data stored in the Apple iPhone 7 Plus which needs to be transferred to iPhone 8/11/11 Pro.

Step 5. Follow the steps given above to view the options with respect to the new iPhone 8/11/11 Pro on the iTunes interface. Select “**Restore Backup**” and waiter the process to get over to transfer data from old iPhone to iPhone 8/11/11 Pro.
