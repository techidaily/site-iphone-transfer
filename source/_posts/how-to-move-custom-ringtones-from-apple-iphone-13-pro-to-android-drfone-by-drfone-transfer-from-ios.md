1. **Can I use the same eSIM card to switch between different Apple iPhone 13 Pro and other iPhones?**

Yes, you can use the same eSIM card to switch between different Apple iPhone 13 Pro and other iPhones, provided that the specific carrier supports eSIM technology and allows the transfer of the eSIM between devices.
